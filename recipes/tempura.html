Tempura Recipe

Tempura is a dish involving ingredients like seafood, meat, and vegetables covered in batter and deep-fried in oil.

The batter usually contains flour and egg. Tempura is generally dipped in a special sauce called tentsuyu before eating.

Tentsuyu is a sauce made of broth from kombu or dried bonito, mirin, and soy sauce mixed at a ratio of 4:1:1 and cooked. You can add ginger or grated radish to your liking for a more refreshing taste.

Ingredients

  • 4 shrimp
  • 1 Japanese/Chinese eggplant
  • 2 king oyster mushrooms
  • 2 shiso leaves
  • 4 slices lotus root
  • 4 slices Japanese sweet potato
  • 4 slices kabocha
  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour

Recipe Instructions

  1. Clean the ingredients
  2. Prepare them for deep frying
  3. Deep fry everything after you cover them in batter
