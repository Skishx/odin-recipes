Sushi Recipe

Sushi is one of the best known Japanese foods around the world.

Sushi usually refers to a dish of pressed vinegared rice with a piece of raw fish or shellfish, called a neta, on top.

Sushi is generally eaten with soy sauce and wasabi, but those who aren't too fond of wasabi can ask for "sabi-nuki"

Ingredients
  • sushi rice
  • bamboo sushi mat
  • plastic wrap
  • nori seaweed sheets

Recipe Instructions

  1. Prep the Rice & Nori. Lay out the nori on the bamboo mat.
  2. Spread the Rice on the Nori.
  3. Add Fillings to the Bottom Quarter of the Nori.
  4. Roll the Sushi Part Way.
  5. Roll the Sushi the Rest of the Way.
  6. Move to Cutting Board.
  7. Cut the Sush
  8. Serve.
